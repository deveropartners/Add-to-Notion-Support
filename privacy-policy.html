Privacy Policy

Add to Notion for macOS and iOS — Effective March 31, 2026

Overview

Add to Notion is a lightweight client for the Notion API that lets you quickly add tasks and manage pages from your menu bar (macOS) or home screen (iOS). Your privacy is fundamental to how this app is built: it connects directly to Notion's servers using your own API key, with no intermediary servers, no analytics, and no tracking.

What Data the App Uses

Add to Notion handles only the data necessary to function:

  • Notion API Key — The internal integration token you provide to authenticate with Notion.
  • Notion Workspace Data — Database names, page titles, property values, and other content fetched from your Notion workspace via the Notion API.
  • App Preferences — Your selected databases, favorites, filter settings, and display preferences.

Where Data Is Stored

All data remains on your device:

  • API Key — Stored in your device's Keychain using Apple's Security framework. The key is local to the device and is not synced via iCloud Keychain or any other service. On the same device, the key is shared between the main app and its extensions (share extension) through a secure Keychain access group.
  • App Preferences — Your selected databases, favorites, filter settings, and display preferences are stored in a local file on your device. These are not synced or uploaded anywhere.
  • Cached Task Data — The app caches a summary of your Notion database items (titles, status, dates, and property values) in local files on your device. This cache enables the iOS widget to display your items and allows the app to show data while loading. Cached data is not synced or uploaded anywhere.
  • Recent Items — A list of recently added items (title, database, and timestamp) is stored locally for your convenience. This data is not synced or uploaded anywhere.

Network Connections

The app communicates with exactly one external service: Notion's API at api.notion.com. All requests use HTTPS and are authenticated with your API key. The app does not contact any other servers, does not load remote resources, and does not include any analytics, crash reporting, or telemetry services.

Data We Do Not Collect

To be explicit about what does not happen:

  • No data is sent to the developer or any third party.
  • No analytics, usage tracking, or event logging occurs.
  • No crash reports are transmitted.
  • No device information, identifiers, or location data is collected.
  • No advertising frameworks or tracking SDKs are included.
  • No accounts are created; the app uses your existing Notion integration token.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service the app interacts with is Notion (Notion's Privacy Policy). Your use of the Notion API is subject to Notion's own terms and privacy policy. The developer of Add to Notion has no access to your Notion account or data.

Children's Privacy

Add to Notion is not directed at children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.
